HOW BRIDGE SCOUT WORKS

What is Bridge Scout?

Bridge Scout is a crosschain bridge aggregator that automatically finds the cheapest, fastest, and most secure route to transfer tokens between networks like Ethereum, Arbitrum, Optimism, Polygon, Base, and others.

How it Works

1. Connect Your Wallet

Connect MetaMask, Trust Wallet, Rabby, or any Web3 wallet. No seed phrase required ‚Äî only smart contract connection.

2. Select Networks & Token

Choose which network you're sending from and which network you're sending to (e.g., Ethereum ‚Üí Arbitrum, USDT).

3. Automatic Bridge Scanning

Bridge Scout checks top bridges including Stargate, Hop Protocol, Across, Synapse, Rhino, and more. Compares fees, speed, and reliability.

4. Best Route Recommendation

Platform shows you the optimal route with estimated time, gas cost, slippage, and total fee.

5. Execute Bridge

Click "Bridge Now" and confirm the transaction through your wallet. Transfer is executed via the selected bridge.
